Director of Statistics .The Tobacco Institute Title: Epidemic by the Numbers
Dr. Marvin A. Kastenbaum is currently the director of statistics at the Tobacco Institute in Washington, D.C. Prior to assuming -this position he was with the department of statistics at Stanford. He has also been on the faculty of the University of Wisconsin and has worked for Oak Ridge National Laboratory, the Institute of Human Biology at the in University of Michigan, and thc Atomic Bomb Casualty Commission. He is a fellow of ASA, the Royal Statistical Society, and American Association for the Advancement of Science.
